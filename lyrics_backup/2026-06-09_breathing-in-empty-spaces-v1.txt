[Hook]
Lost in the glow of a faded porch light
Waiting for your shadow to step through the night
Every breath I take is calling your name
Pulling you close like a moth to a flame

[Verse 1]
Dust on the dashboard and miles on the road
Carrying secrets that nobody knows
I left the porch swing swaying in the breeze
Hoping tonight you will fall back to me

[Pre-Chorus]
The stars are aligning, the fire is low
I feel your touch everywhere that I go
The silence is loud but it’s singing our tune
Under the spell of a midnight moon

[Chorus]
Breathing in empty spaces without you here
Whispering words that I want you to hear
Trading my lonely for your warm embrace
Lost in the rhythm of this quiet place

[Post-Chorus]
Falling so deep in the midnight blue
Breathing in empty spaces for you

[Verse 2]
Shadows are dancing across the wood floor
I leave a light on and unlock the door
Counting the seconds until you arrive
You are the spark that makes me alive

[Pre-Chorus 2]

I feel your touch everywhere that I go
The silence is loud but it’s singing our tune
Under the spell of a midnight moon

[Chorus]
Breathing in empty spaces without you here
Whispering words that I want you to hear
Trading my lonely for your warm embrace
Lost in the rhythm of this quiet place

[Post-Chorus 2]
Falling so deep in the midnight blue
Breathing in empty spaces for you

[Bridge]
I surrender my walls, let the wild horses run
Two lonely drifters now blazing like the sun
Every shadow of sorrow is fading to blue
I am pouring my soul out in all that I do
Washing away all the rust and the pain
The heart of Saeed only calls out your name

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (Come a little closer to the fire)
[Spoken] (Let me pull you slightly higher)

[Final Chorus]
Breathing in empty spaces, now you are near
Whispering promises into your ear
Trading my lonely for your warm embrace
Finding my soul in this beautiful place
